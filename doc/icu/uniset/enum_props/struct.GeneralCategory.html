#[repr(transparent)]
pub struct GeneralCategory(_);
Expand description

Enumerated Unicode general category types. The discriminants correspond to the U_GC_XX_MASK constants in ICU4C. Unlike GeneralSubcategory, this supports groups of general categories: for example, Letter is the union of UppercaseLetter, LowercaseLetter, etc… See https://www.unicode.org/reports/tr44/ . See UCharCategory and U_GET_GC_MASK in ICU4C.
